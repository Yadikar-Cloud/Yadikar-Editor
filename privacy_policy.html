Data Collection and Usage

Yadikar Text Editor is designed with privacy in mind. The application processes your documents locally on your device. We do not automatically collect, store, or transmit your document content to external servers unless you explicitly choose to use cloud synchronization or sharing features.

When you use Yadikar Text Editor, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Application usage statistics (features used, performance metrics)
  • Device information (operating system, app version)
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve application stability
  • Optional account information if you create an account for cloud features

Document Storage and Privacy

Your documents are stored locally on your device by default. Yadikar Text Editor does not access, read, or transmit your document content without your explicit consent. If you choose to enable cloud backup or synchronization features, your documents will be encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

Log Files and Analytics

Yadikar Text Editor may collect anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the application. This information may include application version, operating system, feature usage patterns, and performance metrics. This data is aggregated and does not contain any personally identifiable information or document content.

Cookies and Local Storage

Yadikar Text Editor may use local storage mechanisms to save your preferences, settings, and application state. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. If you use the web version of Yadikar Text Editor, standard cookies may be used to maintain your session and preferences.

Third-Party Services

Yadikar Text Editor may integrate with third-party services (such as cloud storage providers) if you choose to enable these features. When using third-party integrations, you will be subject to the privacy policies of those third-party services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before enabling these features.

Note that Yadikar has no access to or control over cookies or data collected by third-party services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Information
